About Us

Launched in 2019 under Cap Food Products, Cafecap quickly gained the trust of true coffee lovers across Kottayam, Idukki, and Pathanamthitta districts. With a turnover of ₹3.5 crore and counting, we are passionate about serving black coffee connoisseurs with a taste that stands out.

Sourcing premium Arabica and Robusta beans directly from farmers in Kerala's renowned coffee-growing regions, we ensure no compromises on quality. Our coffee powder is uniquely crafted to deliver a rich, smooth taste that elevates everyday moments.

Our Coffee - A Promise Purity

Every batch of Cafecap coffee is made from handpicked Arabica plantations, Robusta AA, and Robusta PB beans — all premium-grade. Our processing standards meet the globally recognized ISO 22000:2018 food safety management system, ensuring unmatched quality

By working directly with farmers in Idukki and Wayanad, we uphold the highest standards of sustainability and freshness. From plantation to processing, every step is meticulously controlled to deliver coffee that is pure, potent, and full of character.

A Strong Backbone - Our Partnership with HRCC

Cafecap sources premium green coffee beans through a trusted supply chain, partnering with High Range Coffee Curing Pvt Ltd (HRCC), a leading name in Indian coffee processing since 1995.

HRCC is renowned for its excellence, holding certifications from the Coffee Board of India and Rainforest Alliance, with accreditations by Nestlé India. With state-of-the-art facilities and decades of experience, HRCC ensures that only the finest beans reach Cafecap, guaranteeing exceptional quality for our customers.
